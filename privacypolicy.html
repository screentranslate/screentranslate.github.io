Privacy Policy

Red Screen Translator

Red Screen Translator is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles information when you use our services.

Permissions Required and Their Purposes

Display Over Other Apps

This permission allows the application to appear on top of other apps, enabling instant translation without interrupting your current activity.

Screen Capture or Accessibility Permission

Screen translation requires Screen Capture or Accessibility permission to recognize text displayed on your screen.

Captured screen content is used solely for text recognition and translation. Images and extracted text are not stored, shared, or used for any other purpose.

Camera and Microphone Permissions

  • Camera Permission: Required for Camera Translation.
  • Microphone Permission: Required for Voice Translation.

These permissions are only used when you actively access the corresponding features.

Information We Collect

Red Screen Translator does not collect, store, or share personal information from users.

Some integrated third-party services may collect limited technical or usage information for analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or payment processing.

Third-Party Services

  • Google ML Kit Text Recognition & Tesseract OCR
    Text recognition from screenshots and images.
  • Firebase Analytics & Microsoft Analytics
    App usage analytics and performance monitoring.
  • Firebase Crashlytics & Microsoft Crashlytics
    Crash detection and bug reporting.
  • Google AdMob & Facebook Audience Network
    Advertising services within the application.

Cookies

Red Screen Translator does not directly use cookies.

However, certain third-party services may use cookies or similar technologies to improve functionality, analytics, or advertising services.

External Links

The application may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of external websites.

We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites they visit.

Children's Privacy

Red Screen Translator is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and become effective immediately after publication.
